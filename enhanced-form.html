Biomedical Engineering Department

Hallelujah General Hospital – Ensuring safe, effective, and well-maintained medical equipment for optimal patient care.

Department Responsibilities

  • Routine maintenance and calibration of medical equipment
  • Repair of faulty devices and systems
  • Equipment safety checks and compliance inspections
  • Training clinical staff on proper equipment use
  • Procurement and evaluation of new medical technologies
